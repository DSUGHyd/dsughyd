DevOps & SRE User Group, Hyderabad

A Community Driven Group to bring all DevOps & SRE professionals at a common platform and learn, share & collaborate with the larger community.

Who we are?

Welcome to DevOps & SRE User Group Hyderabad!!

Purpose - This group is for anyone who is interested to learn and collaborate about DevOps & SRE in general. We plan to bring all professional DevOps & SRE professionals to the same platform and help the community learn and grow together.

Who Shall join - Anyone who has the interest to learn DevOps or already working on DevOps or would like to share his/her knowledge with the community

What we will do - We plan to organize regular meetups and workshops to learn and collaborate within the community and there would be special emphasis on "Learning from real-time use cases".

if you are interested in being a speaker at one of our meetups, hosting an event, or volunteering with us please feel free to write at contact@dsughyd.org or ping us on telegram group shared below.
